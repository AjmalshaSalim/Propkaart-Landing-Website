About Godrej Lakeside Orchard - Sarjapur Road

Introducing Godrej Lakeside Orchard at Sarjapur Road: Godrej Lakeside Orchard is a newly launched residential landmark in Godrej Park Retreat at Sarjapur Main Road, Bangalore. It offers a range of 2, 3, 3.5 & 4.5 BHK with scenic views of the Hadosiddapura Lake and sprawling greenery. With only 4 flats on every floor and private balconies, you are assured of seamless comfort and privacy. Each unit is smartly designed with minimum common walls to ensure uninterrupted views, ventilation, and natural light. The community features 6 acres of open green space with orchards, 700+ trees, 1.9 acres of podium green spaces, etc. The 40+ amenities include a gymnasium, butterfly park, amphitheatre, reflexology walkway, basketball court, cafe, outdoor working space, children’s play area, swimming pool and more.

Your home at Godrej Lakeside Orchard is strategically located near prominent facilities. Delhi Public School, CMR Gandhi Public School, Harvest International School, RGA Tech Park, Natus Women & Children Hospital, Manipal Hospital, Nexus Mall, RMZ Ecospace, Dmart, Soul Space Spirit Centro Mall, Global Technology Park, and Bier Library are just a few minutes of reach. The Sarjapur Main Road and Outer Ring Road are the vital source of connectivity across the city. The Carmelaram railway station and the forthcoming Carmelaram metro station are just a short drive. So, discover an elevated lifestyle with nearby conveniences at Godrej Lakeside Orchard in Bangalore.
